# Policy and Compliance Basics

## What Are Security Policies?

Policies define acceptable use and security expectations.

Examples:
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Password Policy
- Access Control Policy

---

## Compliance Frameworks

Examples:
- ISO 27001
- NIST
- GDPR

---

## Why Compliance Matters

Compliance ensures:
- Legal protection
- Risk reduction
- Standardized security practices

SOC analysts must operate within defined policies and procedures.
