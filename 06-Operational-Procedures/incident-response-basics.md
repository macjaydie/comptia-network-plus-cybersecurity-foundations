# Incident Response Basics

## What is an Incident?

A security incident is an event that threatens the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of systems or data.

Examples:
- Malware infection
- Unauthorized access
- Data exfiltration

---

## Incident Response Phases

1. Preparation
2. Identification
3. Containment
4. Eradication
5. Recovery
6. Lessons Learned

---

## Role of a SOC Analyst

SOC analysts:
- Monitor alerts
- Validate incidents
- Escalate threats
- Document findings

---

## Importance of Timely Response

Delayed response increases:
- Damage
- Downtime
- Recovery costs
