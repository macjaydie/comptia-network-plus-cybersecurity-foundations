# Email Protocols

## SMTP (Port 25)

Used for sending email.

---

## POP3 (Port 110)

Downloads emails to local device.

---

## IMAP (Port 143)

Synchronizes emails across devices.

---

## Secure Versions

SMTPS – 465  
IMAPS – 993  
POP3S – 995  

---

## Security Risks

- Phishing campaigns
- Email spoofing
- Spam
- Malware attachments

SOC analysts review mail gateway logs for suspicious activity.
