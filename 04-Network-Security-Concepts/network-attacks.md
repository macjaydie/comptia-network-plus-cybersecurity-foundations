# Common Network Attacks

## Denial of Service (DoS)

Overwhelms systems to disrupt availability.

---

## Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)

Uses multiple systems to launch a DoS attack.

---

## Man-in-the-Middle (MitM)

Intercepts communication between two parties.

---

## ARP Spoofing

Attacker sends fake ARP messages to redirect traffic.

---

## DNS Poisoning

Corrupts DNS cache to redirect users to malicious sites.

---

## Security Importance

Understanding attack methods helps SOC analysts:
- Detect anomalies
- Investigate alerts
- Correlate logs
